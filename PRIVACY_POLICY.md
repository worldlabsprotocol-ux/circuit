PRIVACY POLICY

Circuit values user privacy.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

Data collected may include:

• Public wallet addresses
• On-chain transaction references
• Anonymous usage analytics
• Microphone permission access when explicitly enabled
• Device metadata for performance optimization

Audio recordings remain local unless the user explicitly shares, uploads, or mints content.

Circuit does not access or store private keys.

Wallet interactions occur via secure wallet providers or Solana Mobile’s Seed Vault secure hardware layer.

Blockchain transactions are public by design. Circuit is not responsible for publicly visible blockchain metadata once submitted.

ROYALTY & SMART CONTRACT DISCLAIMER

Circuit provides smart contract infrastructure for automated royalty splits.

Smart contracts are deployed on the Solana blockchain and operate autonomously once executed.

Circuit does not guarantee:

Users are responsible for reviewing and approving all royalty configurations before minting.

All blockchain activity is final.
